# Roseto Group Flower Catalogue 2026

## Overview

This repository contains the official 2026 flower catalogue for Roseto Group. It provides a comprehensive overview of all flower varieties produced across the group’s four farms, based on planned and actual production for the year.

The catalogue was prepared in conjunction with the IfTex Exhibition held in Naivasha in May 2026.

## Farms Included

The catalogue covers the following Roseto Group farms:

* **Roseto Farm** – Nakuru (Salgaa)
* **Sierra Farm** – Elburgon (Molo)
* **Florenza Solai** – Solai
* **Florenza Rongai** – Rongai–Salgaa Area

## Contents

Within this repository, you will find:

* A full list of flower varieties per farm
* Production-based classification for the year 2026
* Organized data for easy reference and planning

## Purpose

This catalogue is intended to:

* Showcase Roseto Group’s production capacity and variety range
* Support clients, partners, and stakeholders in selection and planning
* Serve as a reference during and after the IfTex 2026 exhibition

## Usage

You are free to browse and use this catalogue for informational and business purposes. For commercial inquiries or partnership opportunities, please contact Roseto Group directly.

## Notes

* All data reflects 2026 production projections and availability.
* Availability of specific varieties may vary due to seasonal or operational factors.
